Alcala Milk

Ingredients

  • Carabao Milk
  • White sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine carabao milk with sugar
  2. Boil it until it thickens
  3. Wait for it to cool down
  4. Use rolling pins or other instruments to flatten out the candy
  5. Chill in the fridge, serve and enjoy!
